About ApexStreet

Redefining street fashion since 2020, bringing you the perfect blend of style and comfort.

Our Mission

ApexStreet's objective is to become a leading and recognized goods store in Malaysia, producing great products that interact with our customers and establish trends in the industry.

Our Vision

To revolutionize the fashion industry in Malaysia by delivering innovative designs that bridge the gap between street style and professional wear.

Our Journey

  1. 2020: The Beginning

    Founded with a vision to revolutionize street fashion in Malaysia

  2. 2021: Expansion

    Expanded our collection to include professional wear with street style elements

  3. 2022: Partnerships

    Established partnerships with local designers and sustainable manufacturers

  4. 2023: Digital Growth

    Launched our online platform to reach fashion enthusiasts nationwide

  5. 2024: Sustainability

    Introduced our sustainable fashion line using eco-friendly materials
